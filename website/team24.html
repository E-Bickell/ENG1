Heslington Hustle

What is it?

Heslington Hustle is a desktop game in which players live out the life of a York Computer Science student in the last week before their exams.

How to Play

Players must go about their student life preparing for exams. They have to make sure they get a good balance of studying and leisure, mainting a healthy life for their student, until the end of the week.

How to use

The game is designed for desktop, so can be installed and played on any computer.

Team 24

This game was created by team 24: Alex Dollard, Donovan McGraph, Eleanor Bickell, Isaac Dunn, Olivia Hutchison and Robbie Toombs. Why 24? The prime factors of 24 are 2/\3*3 which is interesting as the power of two is equivalent to the next factor. It is also the number our team was initially assigned. Finally, 24 is an abbreviated way of saying the year we are currently in.
